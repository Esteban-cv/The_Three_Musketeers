The Birth of an Alliance

In the lavish Crystal Empire, three destinies collided. An ambitious monster, an immortal warrior, and an oracle of truth were all snubbed by the same princess. Humiliated but not defeated, they met at the Inn of the Last Resort. There, amid the echoes of their failures, they swore loyalty not to a king, but to each other, forging a pact stronger than any crown.
