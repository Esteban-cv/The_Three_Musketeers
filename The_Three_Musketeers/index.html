Mind, Fire, and Truth. The alliance an empire should never have rejected. Three rejected souls who found their destiny in unity and forged an empire from failure.

Forged in rejection

In the lavish Crystal Empire, three destinies crossed...

The great tournament

Princess Lumina, whose beauty eclipses the sun, called a tournament to find a consort. Heroes from across the kingdom responded.

The tests

Mike Rodriguez arrived with grandiose plans. Karmelo demonstrated unparalleled courage. Chikapú certified honesty like a Royal Oracle.

The triple rejection

Mike: rejected for "exaggerated ambition." Karmelo: discarded for "brutal insistence." Chikapú: banished for revealing the truth with an Electric Fact.

The Three Musketeers

           

Meet the spurned heroes who forged their own destiny

         
       
       
         
           
                           

Mike Rodriguez

             
The Ambitious Mind
             

                Mike is the master strategist, the visionary whose monstrous ambition became his greatest strength.                 Rejected for his "too grandiose" plans, he transformed every "no" into fuel to create                 the empire everyone needed but no one dared to imagine.              

           
         
         
           
                           

Karmelo

             
The Resilient Phoenix
             

                Karmelo comes from a lineage of fighters who always fell in battle, but he broke tradition.                 His "Fiery Rebirth" allows him to disintegrate into burning dust and return stronger.                 Every defeat is just the prelude to a more powerful rebirth.              

           
         
         
           
                           

Chikapú

             
The Lightning Bolt of Truth
             

                Chikapú was born in a world where lies were the norm. His "Electric Fact" consists of releasing truths                 that shake like lightning, illuminating hidden reality. He doesn't fight with physical force, but with the power                 of truth that cleanses the air like thunder in a storm.

The Fourth Musketeer

           
             
An Open Invitation
             

                The legend says that Mike, Karmelo, and Chikapú have always believed their council needs a fourth member.                 Someone who, like them, has been rejected for being "too much" but possesses a unique quality that                 complements their perfect trio.              

             

                Would you be that fourth musketeer? What special quality would you bring to the Domain of Authenticity?                 The story remains open, waiting for someone else to join this alliance of outcasts who                 changed the world.
